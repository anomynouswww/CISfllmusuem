Owo Ivory Carvings

Welcome to the CIS Owo Ivory Carvings!!

The ivory carvings of Owo tell stories of royalty and spiritual power. Made from elephant tusks, these delicate yet powerful artworks were used in royal courts and sacred rituals. Some carvings show rulers surrounded by attendants, symbolizing authority and divine connection. Because ivory was rare and valuable, owning such carvings showed great status. Each piece feels like a frozen moment of court
