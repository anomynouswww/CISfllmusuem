Igbo Ukwu Bronzes

The Igbo Ukwu Bronzes reveal a hidden world of wealth and ceremony. When they were discovered, people were amazed at the level of detail—tiny ropes, patterns, and designs cast perfectly in metal. These objects were likely used in religious rituals or buried with important individuals. Their discovery changed history, proving that ancient Igbo society had advanced technology and far-reaching trade connections long before it was widely recognized. 5. Owo Ivory Carvings The ivory carvings of Owo tell stories of royalty and spiritual power. Made from elephant tusks, these delicate yet powerful artworks were used in royal courts and sacred rituals. Some carvings show rulers surrounded by attendants, symbolizing authority and divine connection. Because ivory was rare and valuable, owning such carvings showed great status.
