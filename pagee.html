Nok Terracotta Figures

Welcome to the CIS Nok Terracotta Figures!!

The Nok Terracotta figures feel almost mysterious, like voices from a forgotten civilization. Discovered by accident during mining, these clay figures date back over 2,000 years. Many are broken, leaving archaeologists to guess their full meaning. Their large heads and expressive faces suggest they may have represented ancestors or spirits. It’s as if the Nok people left behind fragments of a story we are still trying to understand today.
