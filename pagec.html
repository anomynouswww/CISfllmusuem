Benin Bronzes

Welcome to the CIS FLLL BENIN BRONZES PAGE !!

The famous Benin Bronzes are more than just metal artworks they are storytellers in bronze. Created in the powerful Benin Kingdom, these plaques once covered the walls of the Oba’s palace, almost like a historical record carved in metal. They show kings, warriors, and important ceremonies, preserving moments of victory and tradition. When British forces invaded Benin in 1897, many of these works were taken away, scattering pieces of the kingdom’s history across the world. Today, their return represents not just art coming home, but history being restored. .
